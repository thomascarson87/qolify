# Qolify — Product

---

## The Problem

Buying a home is the most significant financial and emotional decision of a person's life. Yet it is currently made with dangerously incomplete information.

Standard Spanish property portals — Idealista, Fotocasa, Pisos.com — deliver **Surface Data**: price, square metres, and photos. They tell you what the walls look like. They do not tell you:

- Whether the building has a pending structural inspection failure (ITE) that will cost €15,000+ in levies six months after you move in
- Whether the postcode sits inside a designated flood zone (SNCZI) that will make mortgage insurance unaffordable
- Whether the asking price is 20% above the Catastro Valor de Referencia — meaning you pay tax on a higher value than you paid
- Whether a new AVE station 2km away has just been approved, making the area the most undervalued in the province
- Whether the building is 70% short-term tourist lets (VUTs) — no real neighbours, can't sleep on weekends
- Whether a government ICO guarantee means you could buy with near-zero deposit — a life-changing fact Idealista will never surface

This information asymmetry does not affect institutional investors and large funds. They have access to all of it. It affects families, first-time buyers, retirees, and individuals — the people for whom the stakes are highest.

---

## The Solution

Qolify is a **property intelligence platform** for the Spanish market. It aggregates government, environmental, social, and market data to produce a complete picture of a property's true quality of life and value.

The platform operates across two complementary modes:

**Model B — On-Demand Analysis (Free + Pro)**
User finds a property on Idealista, pastes the URL (or clicks the browser extension). Qolify fetches that listing, enriches it with all available data, runs 15 composite indicators, and returns a full Hidden DNA report in under 10 seconds. No bulk scraping. No stored listing database. The intelligence layer is the product.

**Model A — Discovery Portal (Explorer + Intelligence)**
Qolify scrapes all major Spanish portals continuously, stores the national listing database, enriches every property in the background, and presents them through a map-based interface with composite indicator filters and heatmap overlays. Users discover properties on Qolify without visiting Idealista.

Both models share the same QoL reference data and composite indicator engine. Model B is built first and validated before Model A infrastructure is introduced.

## The Value Proposition

Three things no competitor offers together:

**1. Data Depth**
Government APIs, municipal portals, regional registries, and commercial sources that no mainstream portal aggregates. Flood risk. Building health. School catchments. Fibre coverage. Crime trends. Future infrastructure approvals. All in one place, for any property in Spain.

**2. Adaptive Filtering**
Every data point is weighted and filtered based on what matters to the individual user. The same property database serves a retired couple, a remote-working family, and a yield investor differently — because the same house is a different asset depending on who you are.

**3. Transparent Value Intelligence**
The Negotiation Gap (asking vs Catastro tax value). The Financing Gap (ICO eligibility). Structural Risk (ITE status). Future Value (approved infrastructure). Each of these individually can change the true cost of a property by tens of thousands of euros. Together, they represent institutional-grade intelligence delivered to an individual buyer.

## Target User

Any person considering a residential property purchase in Spain.

The platform does not lock users into fixed persona types. It provides a comprehensive filter system that any user can configure to reflect their priorities. For design and weighting purposes, four primary usage modes are identified:

| Mode | Typical user | Primary data concerns |
|---|---|---|
| Young Family | First-time buyer, under 35, limited deposit | Schools, safety, ICO eligibility, green space |
| Digital Nomad | Remote worker, flexible location | Fibre coverage, airport access, community, cost |
| Retiree | Downsizing or relocating | Health centre proximity, noise, flat terrain, stability |
| Yield Investor | Buying to let or flip | Rental yield, price velocity, rent cap zones, liquidity |

These are **filter presets**, not separate products. Any user can customise weights freely.

## What Qolify is Not

- Not a property portal. We do not take listings fees or serve sellers.
- Not a mortgage broker. We surface ICO eligibility and financing intelligence, but do not sell mortgages.
- Not a real estate agent. We give buyers intelligence to act independently.
- Not a replacement for professional legal and financial advice. We surface risk flags; users consult their own advisors.
